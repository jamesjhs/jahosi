What this page helps with

SocialQA helps make adult social care funding easier to understand. It is designed for questions about care home fees, nursing home costs, NHS Continuing Healthcare, NHS-funded nursing care, financial assessments, care assessments, complaints and finding regulated care services.

Answers are written in plain English and should point back to official or professional references so the original information can be checked.

Important disclaimer

SocialQA is an experimental AI-assisted information page. AI responses can be incomplete, outdated, misread a source, or phrase something too strongly. Do not rely on an answer as the sole basis for a care, funding, legal, financial or medical decision.

  • Always open the cited references and check the original official wording yourself.
  • Confirm important decisions with the relevant local authority, NHS integrated care board, care provider, regulator, ombudsman, qualified adviser or professional representative.
