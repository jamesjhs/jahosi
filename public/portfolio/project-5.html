Museum Volunteer Management System

A TypeScript/Next.JS, React, Tailwind and Prisma (database)-powered website designed to make my life easier for Volunteer Coordinators at Museums or Volunteer-led organisations, this project was borne of the simple need to organise members more effectively. I had the simple self-defined brief: create a method by which staff, trustees, team leaders and volunteers could be structured and managed under one site.

The goal is integration with current Sharepoint/Azure webservers, but this proof-of-concept model has been created iteratively over many days to encompass the 'barebones' of the system to hopefully drive the museum into the future. This project would have been impossible to do in such a short period of time without the offerings of GitHub/Copilot!
