Project

Leccy

EV charging cost tracker · Full-stack PWA · v1.1.1

Leccy came out of frustration with the lack of simple tools for tracking the real-world cost of charging an electric vehicle across multiple tariffs and locations. Home off-peak, rapid public chargers, and everything in between — it all adds up differently, and most apps either ignore it or bury it in subscriptions.

The brief: a clean, fast web app that installs as a native-feeling PWA on both Android and iOS, lets you log a session in seconds, and builds up a genuinely useful picture of your charging habits and efficiency over time — including more advanced metrics like battery health proxy, thermal impact on range, and GOM accuracy.

Dashboard — cost per session, efficiency trend, and recent sessions at a glance

Key features

  • Log charging sessions: battery %, range, odometer, temperature, cost, and location
  • Home and public charger cost tracking with tariff management over time
  • Vehicle maintenance log
  • Analytics dashboard: efficiency, cost per session, temperature vs range, and more
  • Advanced analytics: Battery Health Proxy, Thermal Impact, GOM Accuracy, Range Anxiety Gauge, Charging Habits
  • Multi-user support with admin panel
  • PWA — installs full-screen on Android (Chrome) and iOS (Safari)
