Project

splash!

Pool/hot-tub thermal-cost-chemistry planner · Guided quick start · Global weather inputs · v1.7.0

splash! is a planning tool for temporary and lightly installed outdoor pools and hot tubs. It estimates warm-up effort, overnight cooling, likely running cost, and rough maintenance chemistry quantities by combining geometry, heater setup, tariffs, cover assumptions, location-aware weather context, and optional chemistry readings.

The app was designed to bridge a practical gap: many pool calculators are either too generic or too simplistic. splash! now supports two entry paths: a guided quick-start overlay for first-time users, and the full expert control panel for people who want direct access to every variable.

Guided quick-start flow

  1. 1. Explain and capture browser/manual location for weather and climate inputs
  2. 2. Ask pool shape, dimensions, base, shade, target preset, and season dates
  3. 3. Collect pool use, chlorine type, optional readings, heating setup, cover use, and tariff
  4. 4. Recalculate, save locally, then hand off to forecasts, chemistry cards, and chatbot

Core function

splash! helps answer practical questions before you spend: can your heater reach and hold your target range, how much cover strategy changes outcomes, and what annual/season operating cost you should expect under your selected setup.

Key features

  • First-time quick-start overlay with eight guided questions and a visible progress bar.
  • Browser geolocation shortcut with clear explanation, plus manual global place search fallback.
  • Step-by-step setup for pool shape/dimensions, base/shade, target preset, season dates, use case, chemistry, heating system, cover plan, and tariff.
  • Guided completion path that recalculates the planner, saves browser-local config, opens the chemistry bot, and moves the user toward forecast and chemistry resources.
  • Expert-flow control order for maintenance planning (location first, deeper controls later).
  • Manual forecast workflow that always remains editable.
  • Global place search (country/city/town) for weather auto-fill via Open-Meteo geocoding.
  • Main-page “Fetch current forecast” action plus improved mobile options/close controls.
  • Auto-fill of 3-day daily weather plus 36-hour hourly weather for short-range simulation.
  • Location-aware monthly climate profiling with fallback baseline behaviour when archive data is unavailable.
  • Scenario-sensitive chemistry product guidance, including pH variability from TA, CO₂ outgassing, cover use, aeration, and rainfall exposure.
  • 3-day stepped simulation, 36-hour pool temperature chart, and monthly maintenance/cost analysis.
  • Season planner (pool in / pool away dates) with electricity + water-fill cost summary.
  • Local exports (summary/JSON/CSV + chemistry schedule) and browser-side configuration persistence.
  • Dedicated appendices page documenting formulas and validated calculation sources.

What makes it distinct

Compared with common pool-cost calculators, splash! is unusual in combining manual-first control, global weather + climate adaptation, and practical forecast/cost outputs tailored to temporary playpool use. It also keeps outputs transparent with model checks, explicit assumptions, and locally generated exports for planning rather than black-box estimates.
